Frequently Asked Questions '(FAQ)'
This section contains a wealth of information, related to nicheBrains. If you cannot find an answer to your question, make sure to contact your support@nicheBrains.com
How does the platform work?

It's quite simple actually. Mavens are listed based on experience, domain, industry, ratings etc and Companies who need expert guidance on Digital Transformation, can choose the Maven’s displayed based on experience, domain, industry, company, success stories etc., and schedule a call with them. Once both agree on the call schedule, they can get into an audio/video call and company can get their need addressed. Companies can also post a requirement or question and Mavens can clarify the question/requirement using the comments and based on further need, get into a call.

How do I register with nicheBrains?

Go to nicheBrains.ai. Signup as per your choice. You will receive a verification mail to your registered email id. Upon verification you are registered into nicheBrains.

How do I get started to use nicheBrains?

When you login for the first time, go to profile page fill in key information. These are very easy to fill information giving details of your industry, areas of expertise and skills that you are looking. Next upload your photo/intro video, link your PayPal account and billing address. You are ready to start th e interactions.

Why do I need to create profile video?

Profile Video is an easy way to introduce yourself/your company. Its not mandatory, however creating one, can help you save time to introduce yourself/your company.

Why do I need to create Paypal Account? How do I do it?

Currently, nicheBrains supports payment through, only a Paypal account. So, its mandatory to have a Paypal account to transact in nicheBrains. If you don’t have a Paypal account, just go to https://www.paypal.com/welcome/signup and easily create a new Paypal account. Also to receive money into account, the account has to be a Business Account. So Mavens who receive consulting payment need to create a Business Account.

Why is Billing Address Mandatory?

nicheBrains creates electronic Invoice and Receipt documents after every transaction and is sent to the respective user’s email. For statutory reasons, the billing address has to be mentioned in the electronic document.

Do we have an app for nicheBrains?

Currently nicheBrains support only browsers like Chrome and Mozilla Firefox, viewed from laptop.

During Audio/Video call, what happens if connection is interrupted?

When a call is in progress, if connection is interrupted, due to internet or browser closes unexpectedly or any other issue, don’t worry, just refresh the browser or re-open a new browser, login, go back to your calls details under upcoming calls and you will able to join back in call, till the time of the call. Currently, the audio/video calling feature is in beta and there could still be some issues with call and call saving, but we are working towards making it work seamlessly. For any further questions, please reach out to support@nichebrains.com.

How to search for the right Mavens?

In practice, top trending Mavens are displayed based on ratings, on the top bar. Additionally, you can search of Mavens based on any industry or area of expertise or company, using the search option at the top. Further by selecting the Maven, you can view full information about the Maven, like his experience, expertise, skills, rate, article written by them, customer success stories etc.

How do I connect to a Maven?

To connect to a Maven, you can talk to them directly by Scheduling a Call or Post a Question/Requirement and interact with them, through comments section.

How do I Schedule a Call with Maven?

You can Schedule a Call with a Maven, using the ‘Request a Call’ option, either from Homepage (move cursor over the rate of any displayed Maven) or in the view Maven details page. In the Schedule Call page, give the necessary information like call details, duration of call, preferred time slots and payment information and Submit Call Request to schedule the call. An email will also be sent to yours’s and Maven’s registered email account on the call details.

Can I start the call immediately with Maven after scheduling the call?

Yes, you can start the call immediately, if the following has been done:

  1. Maven accepted your call request time slot.
  2. You have made the payment for the call.
  3. Your current time is within 5 mins of the call start time and ‘Join Call’ button is enabled.
How will I know if the Maven has accepted my call request?

When you select the Calls menu from the menu bar, you will have 3 tabs, Call Requests, Upcoming Calls and Call History. Till the Maven accepts the call, your call/s will be under Call Requests. Once the Maven accepts the call request, your call/s will be under Upcoming Calls. Once your call with Maven is complete, your call/s will be under Call History. You will also receive an email once the Maven accepts the call.

How do I make payment for a Scheduled Call?

Once Maven, accepts the call request, your call will be under Upcoming Calls. If you open the calls details, you will find the option to make payment.

Do I have to pay for all Calls?

No, you don’t have to make a payment under the following options:

  1. If any Maven’s profile is marked as Pro bono, it means that Mavens is available for free consultation temporarily. If you select such a Maven for a call, the call is free and you don’t have to make any payment.
  2. At the time of call schedule there is an option for ‘Waiver Call Fees’. If you select that option and Maven accepts your request for a free call, you don’t have to make payment.
  3. At the time of payment, if you have any coupon which covers for the payment, you don’t have to pay for that call. Additionally, if the coupon cost covers only part of your payment, you will only have to pay for the balance amount.
Will I receive any invoice for the payment?

If you have made a payment for the call and after the call is completed, you will receive an email with an electronic invoice. You can also download your invoice from the Calls -> Call History.

How do I talk to the Maven?

Once you have made the payment and if your current time is within 5 mins of the call schedule time, you will provided with a Join Call option, which will connect you to the Maven for a one-on-one audio/video call. Once the Maven too joins the call, you can talk to him.

What happens post call?

Post the call, you can view the details of the call under Calls->Call History. Under Call History you have the options like Meeting Request, Call Details, MOM, Feedback, Payment Status and Logs. You can view the details of the call, saved audio/video files of the call, MOM and any documents posted by the Maven, feedback for you to rate/rated the Maven, payment details and logs for you to view anytime.

What happens if Maven cancels the call?

If Maven Cancels the call, you can either send an email to nicheBrains support and get your refund within 48 hours or schedule another call with the Maven and let him know you already paid for his earlier call and ask him to accept call with paid option, such that you don’t have to re-pay.

What happens if Maven re-schedules the call?

If Maven Re-schedules the call, you can either accept the re-scheduled time slot and proceed with the call or cancel the call and send an email to nicheBrains support and get your refund within 48 hours.

What happens if Maven does not turn up for the call?

If Maven does not turn up at the time of call, report on this call by email to nicheBrains support and get your refund within 48 hours. Support will also respond back to you, within 48 hours as to why the Maven did not attend the call and take any necessary actions.

What happens if I could not make it to a confirmed call?

If you could not make it to a call due to an emergency, send an email to nicheBrains support and get your refund within 48 hours or schedule one more call and let Maven know you already paid for his earlier call and ask him to accept call with paid option, such that you don’t have to re-pay.

How to I Post an Article or Requirement or Question?

By clicking on your name or image icon on the top-right corner, a drop-down with options to, Write an Article, Post a Requirement and Post a Question will be displayed. Choosing any option will take you to the respective page for you to write and post, for all users to view.

How will Mavens interact with me if I Post an Article or Requirement or Question?

Once you post an article or requirement or question, it is visible to all Mavens and Mavens will use the comments section to respond to your article, requirement or question and you can respond back to their comment/s and continue the interaction. To get detailed thoughts Maven will suggest for a call.

How do I become part of a company and add other users?

If you are the owner or admin of the company, you can create your company by clicking on your name or image icon on the top-right corner, a drop-down with option to Create a Company will we displayed. Choosing that option takes you the page to fill in your company details. You can also verify your company by verifying it with your company LinkedIn profile. You are automatically added to the company and you can now add other users to the company.

Does nicheBrains charge any commission connecting me Mavens?

Not at all. We are currently free to access and use and aspire to give you access to a broad pool of quality Mavens. Of course we would never turn down a word of appreciation!

How do I register myself as an Expert/Maven?

Once you have registered and logged in, clicking on your name or image icon on the top-right corner, a drop-down with option to Become an Expert is displayed or in homepage, below success stories, there is a banner Become and Expert. You can choose one of the options and it takes you to Become an Expert page. Once you verify your LinkedIn profile with nicheBrains and fill in some necessary fields you are registered as an Expert.

Once I become a registered Maven, will consumers start reaching out to me?

Once you have verified and registered yourself as a Maven, you have to set-up additional details in you profile to become visible to consumers. Click on your name or image icon on the top-right corner, a drop-down with option to View Profile is displayed. In the View Profile page, you can update your basic information like Designation, TimeZone, Contact Number, Currency and upload profile video too. In the expert settings you need to set up your rate per hour, preferred timings for calls and your experience. Also, you need to setup your Paypal account for payment to credit and debit based on usage. Setting up all these makes your profile visible to Consumers to reach out to you.

Will consumers be able to reach to me?

Based on your skill/knowledge/experience/industry etc. (that is why its important to give maximum information about yourself), the many SMEs/consumers will reach out to you though a request for call. Based on your availability, consumers will schedule for a call with you. Once you accept the call schedule and payment is made by the consumers, 5 minutes before the scheduled call, a ‘Join Call’ option is enabled for you to join and talk to the consumer.

How will I know that a consumer has requested for a call?

Once a consumer requests for a call with you, you will receive an email. You can login to the platform, in the homepage, under Calls menu, from the menu bar, you will have 3 tabs, Call Requests, Upcoming Calls and Call History. Call/s requested by consumers will be under Call Requests -> Received. Once you accept the call request/s, your call/s will be under Upcoming Calls. Once your call with consumer is complete, it will be under Call History. You can view the call details under Upcoming Calls and once Join Call option is enabled you can join the call and talk to the consumer at the scheduled date and time.

What if I want to waiver the payment for a particular call request or consumer?

If you would like to waive off the consultation fee for a particular call request or consumer or temporarily for all customers, you can do it through 2 options:

  1. Firstly, if you want to temporarily, waive off consulting charges for all consumers, in the Profile Settings, under Expert Settings, you can set your actual rate and select the ‘Pro bono’ option. It means that even though your consulting rate is ‘x’, you would temporarily want to do free consulting and ‘Pro bono’ is displayed against your profile for consumer to identify that your currently doing free consulting.
  2. Secondly, at the time of the call schedule, if consumer has chosen the Waiver Fees for this Call option, you will informed that consumer has requested for fee waiver call and on accepting this call request, consulting fees is waived off fees for that particular consumer call.
Can we reach out to consumers only if they request for a call?

Consumers can reach out to you through 3 other options:

  1. Post an Article – You can attract consumers by posting any article that would interest them. You can interact with them through the comments sections and ask them to connect over a call for further discussion/solution.
  2. Post a Requirement – Consumers can also specifically post a requirement. Like articles, requirements will be visible in the same articles list, but hash tagged as requirement to easily identify them. You can again interact with them on the comments section and ask them to connect over a call for further discussion/solution, if it needs a one on one call.
  3. Post a Question – Consumers can also have some small questions which they can post similar to the article/requirement and hash tagged as question. You can again interact with them on the comments section and ask them to connect over a call for further discussion/solution, if it needs a one on one call.
How and when will I get my payment?

Once your call with the consumer completes, you need to update the MOM, either immediately after the call (you will be prompted for MOM update) or go the Calls -> Call History and update the MOM and load any documents as agreed in call. Once done, within 48 hours you will receive your payment into your registered account.

Will I receive my full consulting rate as payment?

We currently support only Indian Mavens. You will be bound by India's statutory laws. So TDS will be cut on the consulting rate and the balance paid to you. i.e. for e.g. if your consulting rate is Rs. 1000/- per hour and you have done one hour of consulting, by current India Tax norms, 7.5% TDS will be deducted and the balance transferred to you, i.e. Rs. 925/-. We will of course, provide you with the TDS certificate for you to claim the amount based on your Returns Filing. Also PayPal has a commission cut of around 2.5% + INR 3 (Refer Paypal Seller Fees). So for Rs. 925/- you will finally receive around Rs. 899.87/- into your account.

Does nicheBrains charge a percentage of my consultancy fees?

Not at all. We are currently free to access and use and aspire to give you access to a broad pool of quality opportunities. Of course we would never turn down a word of appreciation!

Will I receive any receipt for the payment?

As soon as your payment is made, you will receive an email with an electronic receipt. You can also download your receipt from the Calls -> Call History.
